Our Soil Testing Services

Soil tests analyze the chemical, physical, or biological properties of soil. They can be performed in a lab or in the field, ensuring your soil is suitable for agriculture, construction, or other uses. Explore the different tests we offer below:

This test determines the liquid limit, plastic limit, and shrinkage limit of soil. These values help classify soils and predict their behavior under varying moisture conditions.

This test measures the amount of water in soil. The moisture content is vital in determining if the soil is appropriate for construction, agriculture, or other applications.

This test determines the specific gravity of soil particles, which provides insights into the composition and density of the soil.

This test measures the acidity or alkalinity of soil. Soil pH significantly influences chemical processes and determines the suitability of the soil for various crops.

This test determines the weight and density of dry soil, a crucial metric often used by engineers and builders to assess soil strength and stability.
